Energy Analysis
===============

.. note::
   Convert ``notebooks/02_energy_analysis.py`` to
   ``docs/notebooks/02_energy_analysis.ipynb`` using Jupytext or
   ``jupyter nbconvert``, then this page will render the full notebook.

This notebook covers:

- Computing and decomposing the scHopfield energy into interaction,
  degradation, and bias components
- Grouped boxplots of energy distributions per cell type
- Overlaying energy values on UMAP embeddings
- Computing gene–energy correlations to identify driver genes
- Identifying "corner genes" that are highly specific to individual cell types
