Examples
========

The example notebooks are listed under **User Guide & Examples** in the left
sidebar. They demonstrate scHopfield's capabilities on a hematopoiesis dataset
(Paul et al. 2015) and cover:

- :doc:`Getting started <notebooks/01_getting_started>`: data loading, sigmoid fitting, network inference
- :doc:`Energy analysis <notebooks/02_energy_analysis>`: computing and visualising energy landscapes
- :doc:`Network analysis <notebooks/03_network_analysis>`: centrality metrics, eigenanalysis, GRN visualization
- :doc:`Stability analysis <notebooks/04_stability_analysis>`: Jacobian computation, eigenvalue spectra
- :doc:`Perturbation analysis <notebooks/05_perturbation_analysis>`: in-silico gene knockouts and ODE simulation
- :doc:`Lineage drivers <notebooks/06_lineage_drivers>` and :doc:`extended perturbation <notebooks/07_perturbation_extended_analyses>`
